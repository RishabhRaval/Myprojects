Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all that orbits the Sun. This includes the eight planets and their mooons , the dwarf planets , asteroids , comets and other small objects .

All the planets and Dwarf planets , the rocky asteroids , and the icy bodies in the kuiper belt move around the Sun in elleptical orbits in the same directions that the sun rotates . This motion is termed prograde , or direct motion .
